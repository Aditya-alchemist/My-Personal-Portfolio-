# **Blockchain Portfolio Design Prompt - Brittany Chiang Style**

Based on your previous requirements and the sleek design aesthetic of Brittany Chiang's portfolio, here's a comprehensive prompt that merges both styles:

## **Complete Design Prompt**

**"Create a sophisticated blockchain developer portfolio website inspired by Brittany Chiang's clean, minimalist design aesthetic combined with dark crypto theming:**

### **Overall Layout & Structure**
- **Clean, minimal dark interface** with deep blacks (#0a0a0a), charcoal grays (#1a1a1a), and subtle gradients
- **Fixed navigation bar** with smooth scroll-to-section functionality
- **Hero section** with animated typing effect displaying "Blockchain Developer" or "Web3 Engineer"
- **Numbered sections** (01. About, 02. Experience, 03. Projects, 04. Contact) in Brittany's signature style
- **Vertical email/social links** on the left side (replace with crypto-relevant links)
- **Responsive grid layout** that adapts beautifully across all devices

### **Crypto-Themed Background Animations**
- **Floating cryptocurrency coins** (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Polygon) gently bouncing and rotating in the background
- **Subtle particle network effects** resembling blockchain connections
- **Animated hexagonal patterns** or circuit board traces
- **Glowing neon accents** in electric blue (#00d4ff) and crypto green (#39ff14)
- **Parallax scrolling** with layered blockchain-inspired geometric shapes

### **Section Designs**

**Hero Section:**
- Large, bold typography with your name
- Animated subtitle: "I build decentralized applications for the future"
- Subtle crypto iconography (blockchain symbols, wallet icons)
- Call-to-action button with neon glow effect

**About Section:**
- Clean paragraph layout like Brittany's style
- **Technology stack** displayed as animated skill cards
- Blockchain technologies: Solidity, Web3.js, Ethers.js, Hardhat, Foundry
- Animated progress bars for different blockchain platforms

**Experience Section:**
- **Timeline-based layout** with company logos and descriptions
- **Hover effects** revealing detailed project information
- **Blockchain-specific metrics** (smart contracts deployed, TVL managed, etc.)
- Clean typography with consistent spacing

**Projects Section:**
- **Grid of project cards** with glassmorphism effects
- **Hover animations** revealing live demo and GitHub links
- **Project categories**: DeFi, NFTs, DAOs, Layer 2 solutions
- **Technology tags** for each project (Solidity, React, Node.js)
- **Animated project previews** on hover

### **Color Scheme & Typography**
- **Primary Colors**: Deep black (#0a0a0a), charcoal (#1a1a1a)
- **Accent Colors**: Electric blue (#00d4ff), neon green (#39ff14), purple (#8b5cf6)
- **Text**: Clean white (#ffffff) and light gray (#a1a1aa)
- **Fonts**: Modern sans-serif (Inter, Poppins, or SF Pro Display)
- **Monospace font** for code snippets and wallet addresses

### **Interactive Elements**
- **Smooth scroll animations** triggered on viewport entry
- **Glowing hover effects** on buttons and links
- **Animated counters** for portfolio metrics (projects completed, contracts deployed)
- **Crypto price ticker** in the footer (optional)
- **Dark/light mode toggle** with blockchain-themed icons
- **Loading animations** with cryptocurrency symbols

### **Technical Features**
- **Web3 wallet connection** button in the navigation
- **ENS domain display** if available
- **GitHub contribution graph** styled with crypto colors
- **Blog integration** for blockchain articles[1]
- **Contact form** with Web3 messaging integration
- **Resume download** with blockchain project highlights

### **Mobile Optimization**
- **Hamburger menu** with smooth slide animations
- **Touch-friendly** interactive elements
- **Optimized animations** for mobile performance
- **Responsive cryptocurrency background** that scales appropriately

### **Performance & Accessibility**
- **Optimized animations** that don't impact loading speed
- **Keyboard navigation** support
- **Screen reader friendly** with proper ARIA labels
- **Fast loading times** with optimized assets"**

## **Key Differences from Standard Crypto Portfolios**

This prompt combines Brittany Chiang's **clean, professional aesthetic**[2] with your blockchain requirements, creating a portfolio that stands out in the crypto space by being both sophisticated and technically impressive. The design will appeal to serious blockchain employers while showcasing your technical expertise through both content and implementation[3][4][5][6].

The result will be a portfolio that's **professional enough for traditional tech companies** but **crypto-native enough** to demonstrate your deep understanding of the blockchain ecosystem[7][8][9].
